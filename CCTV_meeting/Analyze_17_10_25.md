## need to know 
    -  Current Situation:  || old -> current transformation or changes 
    -  Business Goals:  || expectation 
    -  The Real Problems

    1.Current Situation 
     - How do customers find you now? (Instagram, word of mouth, walking in?)
     - What happens after they find you on Instagram - do they call, DM, or visit the shop?
     - What frustrates you most about getting new customers or serving existing ones?
     - Do you install CCTVs yourself or just sell them?
    
    2.Business Goals
     - Which makes you more money - selling CCTV products or installation services?
     - Do you want more customers, or better quality customers?
     
    3. Customer Behaviour 
     - What do customers ask before buying? (Price, features, installation cost?)
     - Do they compare your products with competitors?
     - Do they want to see your past work before deciding?

    4. The Real Problems (dig deep here)
     - "You have good Instagram content with customer feedback - but what happens when      someone wants to contact you at 10 PM?"

     - Do you lose customers because they can't reach you immediately?
     - Do people forget about you after seeing your Instagram?
